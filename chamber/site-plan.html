Site Plan: Green Wellness

Site Name

Green Wellness – This name was chosen to reflect a holistic health and wellness brand focused on eco-conscious living, mental clarity, and natural healing. "Green" symbolizes an intentional journey toward sustainable health.

Optional domain: greenpathwellness.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as an informational and interactive resource for individuals interested in improving their physical and mental well-being through eco-conscious practices. It will provide blog articles, guided meditation resources, healthy recipes, and a simple booking system for online wellness coaching.

Scenarios

  • What are some beginner-friendly breathing techniques I can try at home?
  • How do I book a virtual coaching session with a wellness guide?

Color Schema

  • #2e8b57 (Sea Green): Used for headings and accent elements.
  • #f4fdf7 (Mint Cream): Used as the background color for a calming effect.
  • #2e3d30 (Dark Green-Grey): Used for body text for good readability.

Typography

  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text to ensure clarity and modern readability.
  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for headings to add a sense of elegance and personality.
